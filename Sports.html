Soccer

Soccer is a popular world wide and consists of two teams- eleven players on both sides-kicking a ball into the goal.

Soccer Facts

  1. This game was traced back 3000 years ago.
  2. An international game is 90 minutes long.
  3. Other than U.S, soccer is called "football."

American Football

Football is one of the popular sports played in America. It also consists of two teams trying to score on opposite ends.

Football Facts

  1. 600 cows are used to make footballs for a full season.
  2. The Super Bowl is the most watched event.
  3. This sport grew out of rugby and soccer.

Basketball

Basketball is played with two teams and each team tries to make the ball into the hoop. Players have different ways of getting the ball into the hoop like dunking.

  1. The first basketball hoop was a basket.
  2. Basketball was invented in 1891 by James Naismith.
  3. Lenny Wilkins has won more basketball games then any coach in the NBA.
